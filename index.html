About us

Surakji & Shaar was established in December 1999, the head office is located in Nablus. Its new production and logistic center was established in 2011,is located in Huaian Economic and Technological Development Zone with newly built workshop covering an area of about 160,000 square meters and with more than 150 employees.Depend on automatic machines and advanced technology,Jiangsu factory largely expand the production capacity,to provide a strong support and guarantee to our customers. Surakji & Shaar is specialized in producing high quality roller cabinets,chrome vanadium sockets and packaged tool sets.In overseas, Surakji & Shaar reaches more than 50 countries for sales including United States,Germany,Polland,France and so. In 2008, "Magic" brand was set up as the top quality brand name of the company, after 8 years development, Magic has been grown into a famous tool brand which covers the full range of roller cabinets and high quality mechanical tools.In more than 100 countries, Magic has been officially registered, now it is sold in 52 countries,by 390 shops and more. In Surakji & Shaar, all products are all well organized and controlled at every step during designing, producing, packaging and quality controlling in accordance with the international standards.The mission of manufacturing high quality products runs throughout the whole production processes. To create best design,make top quality products ,and build most reputable brands,is our company's goal and mission.We're looking for globle partners who has the same commitment, your kind comments will be highly appreciated by us.
